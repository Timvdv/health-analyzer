Heart qualities

The risk of getting cardiovascular diseases is partially determined by your lifestyle. There are alot of things you can do to reduce the risk of getting cardiovascular diseases, for example: don't smoke, exercise regularly, and keeping a healthy weight.

Important riskfactors:
  • Smoking
  • High Cholesterol
  • High Blood Pressure
  • Overweight
  • Diabetes
  • Heredity
  • Stress

These riskfactors strenghten eachother. If you show signs of multiple riskfactors, you have a bigger chance of getting cardiavascular diseases. Thats why the Doctor always looks at the combination of the riskfactors.
